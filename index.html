About us

A kitchen & coffee house in the heart of Bantry

De Barras is where Bantry starts its morning: proper breakfasts, honest lunches and carefully made coffee, all under one roof on the town’s main street.

Everything is cooked to order, the tables outside catch the morning sun, and the chalkboard changes with the seasons. Drop in — we open at ten.
